Caprese Salad

Embrace simplicity with the classic Caprese Salad. Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze come together to create a light and refreshing dish perfect for any meal.

Discover the timeless elegance of Caprese Salad. This recipe celebrates the harmonious combination of ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Drizzled with a balsamic glaze, this salad is not only a feast for the eyes but also a delightful symphony of flavors that captures the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Finish by drizzling balsamic glaze over the top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the simplicity and freshness of Caprese Salad.

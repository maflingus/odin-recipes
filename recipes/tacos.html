How to Make Tacos

These homemade tacos are quick, easy, and delicious

Ingredients

  • Ground beef – Use lean ground beef for a healthier option
  • Onion – finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves – minced
  • Taco seasoning – store-bought or homemade
  • Tomato sauce – for a richer flavor
  • Olive oil – for sautéing
  • Salt – to taste
  • Pepper – to taste
  • Taco shells – hard or soft, as preferred
  • Shredded lettuce – for topping
  • Chopped tomatoes – for topping
  • Shredded cheese – for topping
  • Sour cream – for topping
  • Salsa – for topping

Recipe

  1. Prep – Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Cook the meat – Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Season – Stir in taco seasoning, tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Assemble – Fill taco shells with the seasoned beef mixture. Top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Serve – Serve the tacos immediately while hot.

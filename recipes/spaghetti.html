How to Make Spaghetti

This classic spaghetti recipe is simple and delicious

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles – Cook according to package instructions
  • Ground beef – Use lean ground beef for a healthier option
  • Onion – finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves – minced
  • Tomato sauce – Use homemade or store-bought
  • Tomato paste – for a richer sauce
  • Olive oil – for sautéing
  • Salt – to taste
  • Pepper – to taste
  • Italian seasoning – for added flavor
  • Parmesan cheese – grated, for serving
  • Fresh basil – chopped, for garnish

Recipe

  1. Prep – Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté – In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook the meat – Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Make the sauce – Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Combine – Add the cooked spaghetti noodles to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Serve – Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Serve hot.
